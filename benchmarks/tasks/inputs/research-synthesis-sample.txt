Research Question: What is the current state of AI adoption in enterprise software development?

Context:
We need to understand how enterprises are currently adopting AI tools for software development, including adoption rates, use cases, benefits realized, and remaining barriers.

Related Questions:
- What percentage of enterprises have adopted AI dev tools?
- Which use cases provide the most value?
- What are the main barriers to adoption?
- How are organizations measuring ROI?
- What security and quality concerns exist?

Time frame: Focus on 2023-2024 developments

Sources to consider:
- Industry surveys (Stack Overflow, Statista, McKinsey)
- Case studies from major tech companies
- Academic research on AI-assisted development
- Enterprise adoption reports
- Security and compliance research

Expected deliverables:
- Summary of adoption rates by company size
- Top use cases and their impact
- Key barriers and risks
- Recommendations for enterprises considering adoption
